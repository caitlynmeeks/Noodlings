Noodlings

Multi-Timescale Affective Agents with Theatrical Control

What are Noodlings?

Noodlings are lightweight neural architectures (~97K parameters) that give conversational AI multi-timescale memory, surprise-driven behavior, and appetite-driven motivation. Unlike traditional chatbots that respond reflexively to every input, Noodlings process experience between messages, creating temporally grounded agents that respond when they have something to say, not just because you spoke.

Built on predictive processing theory and affective neuroscience, Noodlings explore whether hierarchical temporal structure creates qualitatively different agent behavior. We're honest about what we're building: functional correlates of temporal dynamics, not claims of "real" consciousness.

Key Features

Multi-Timescale Processing

Fast/Medium/Slow layers operating at different speeds (seconds, minutes, hours-days)

Surprise-Driven Behavior

Agents predict what happens next and respond when prediction error crosses threshold

Appetite Architecture

Eight core drives (curiosity, status, mastery, novelty, safety, social bond, comfort, autonomy)

Social Cognition

Theory of Mind, relationship modeling, and episodic memory with attention

Theatrical Control (BRENDA)

Natural language scripts become timed phenomenal experiences with millisecond precision

Lightweight & Fast

~97K parameters, runs on Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) with MLX acceleration

Use Cases

  • Interactive Fiction & Gaming: NPCs with persistent memory, emotional arcs, and goal-directed behavior
  • Virtual Assistants: Agents that remember context across conversations and adapt their personality
  • Therapeutic Applications: Empathetic companions that track emotional patterns over time
  • Research & Education: Exploring temporal dynamics in affective architectures and consciousness studies
  • Creative Tools: AI collaborators for writers, game designers, and storytellers needing characters with depth
  • Multi-Agent Simulations: Social worlds where agents form relationships and pursue goals autonomously

Get Started

Noodlings is open-source research code exploring temporal dynamics in affective architectures. Try noodleMUSH, our interactive multi-agent world, or dive into the codebase to build your own applications.

Requirements: Python 3.10+, Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4), macOS 13+, 16GB+ RAM recommended
